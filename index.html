ABOUT gitBEER

Our goal in creating this app is a very simple one: we want to help you (and your friends) find beer near you, no matter where you are. London? No problem. Denver? Easy. Sydney? Yup, we'll find you a brewery there, too.

This app uses The Beer Mapping Project in combination with Google Maps to find you (and get you directions to, if you want!) breweries in any city in the world.

Leave us a comment and let us know what you think of us! Thanks for visiting our app, and happy brewery searching!
